Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Tableau to analyze workforce demographics, performance, income, and attrition.

This project is an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Tableau** to analyze employee data and derive meaningful insights related to workforce demographics, performance, income, overtime, attrition, and overall summary of company's workforce.

## üìà Key Insights from the Dashboard
- The organization has **1,233 active employees**, with **1,470 hires** and **237 terminations**, indicating overall workforce growth.
- The **R&D department** has the highest number of employees, highlighting its strategic importance.
- Gender diversity analysis shows a higher proportion of male employees compared to female employees across departments.
- Employees with higher education levels such as **Master‚Äôs and PhD** tend to earn higher average income.
- Overtime analysis indicates that employees in the **mid-age group** are more likely to work overtime, which may require workload optimization to reduce burnout.
- Most employees fall under **Good** and **Excellent** performance categories, indicating a generally healthy performance culture.
- Income analysis by age and job role shows that **senior roles with higher average age earn higher monthly income**.

---

## üí° Business Recommendations
- Focus on **employee retention strategies**, especially in departments with higher attrition.
- Monitor overtime trends to improve **work-life balance** and reduce burnout risk.
- Encourage **diversity and inclusion initiatives** to improve gender balance.
- Use income and performance insights to support **fair compensation planning**.
